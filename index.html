About the Event

AllOutCricket is an annual cricket celebration that brings together passionate players and fans from across the region. The event is designed to promote the spirit of cricket through competitive matches, interactive fan zones, and family-friendly entertainment. Originally started as a local tournament, it has grown into a major community sports gathering featuring top teams and emerging talent. This event is perfect for die-hard cricket enthusiasts, casual fans, families, and anyone looking to enjoy a day filled with excitement, teamwork, and thrilling performances on the field.
